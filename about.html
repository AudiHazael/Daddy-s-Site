Professor Moses Audi

Professor Moses Audi is a theologian and missiologist with over three decades of academic, pastoral, and missionary engagement. His work bridges rigorous theological scholarship with practical ministry leadership across cultures and contexts.

Academic & Ministerial Credentials

With two doctoral degrees and extensive teaching experience, Professor Audi has served theological institutions, missionary organizations, and church leaders globally. His academic focus integrates biblical theology, missiology, and leadership formation for sustainable Christian witness.

Consulting Focus

  • Strategic theological consultation for churches and institutions
  • Mission strategy development and evaluation
  • Leadership formation for pastors and ministry teams
  • Curriculum design for theological education

Theological Posture & Mission

Professor Audi’s work is grounded in a commitment to biblical faithfulness, contextual relevance, and global mission. His consulting philosophy prioritizes depth over trends, formation over performance, and long-term impact over short-term visibility.
